If your campaign is either "Discrete" or "Staggered", pick a Campaign Budget. The Campaign Budget is a way for you to keep track of how many discounts have been redeemed. A Savvy shopper who redeems a coupon from the app will have dollar discounted value of the product subtracted from the campaign budget. For example, John wants to redeem a Savvy coupon worth 10% off a $100 Dress Shirt. The Savvy Campaign on Dress Shirts has a total budget for %500 for that week. When John redeems the coupon at the till, $10 (10% of $100) gets subtracted from the budget. The budget for the rest of the week is now $490.
